Biography

DJ Beeda SA formaly known as Ben Thaba is a South African Producer, DJ and Media Personality.

Former student of Computer Sciences and once a resident DJ of a community radio station (MCRS FM).

He is the first born of two sons.

Raised for the most part of his early childhood in limpopo and later moved to Mpumalanga.

DJ Beeda SA is the new definition of good house music flava, with a fresh take on what the South African sound represents and an appreciable East African influece.

DJ Beeda SA plans on becoming thee force to reckon with in the main stage of South African Entertainment.

My Music

Having put out his first single at an early stage in his career, and making it to number one on a few charts in local radio stations. The calling was inevitable. With music that navigated it's way from Limpop, Mpumalanga, Gauteng and all the way to west Africa. DJ Beeda RSA continues to release some of the best music and continues to make good waves in Africa and abroad
